Moka Pot

  • Fill the lower chamber with cold water just below the valve. Overfilling will water log the coffee and affect the flavor, so make sure you donâ€™t use too much.
  • Grind your beans to a fine consistency, until you have enough to fill the Moka Pot funnel with coffee.
  • Insert the funnel and fill it with ground coffee. Try not to overfill the strainer with coffee and do not tamp the coffee.
  • Tightly screw the upper part of the pot on to the base.
  • Select a burner size that fits the bottom of the Moka Pot, make sure the flame is not larger than bottom of pot.
  • When the top of the Moka Pot is full of coffee and hazel brown foam begins to appear out of the spout, remove from stove.
  • Pour your coffee into a fancy cup and ta-da!
